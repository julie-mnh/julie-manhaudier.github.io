About

Hi, I'm Julie.

Everything began when I was nine, filming on a Nintendo DSi. Then my phone took over, until at 18 I bought my first real camera. Trips, friends, school projects, whatever was in front of me: I filmed it all and taught myself to edit it into stories. I never really stopped.

I'm French, and a year ago I found a job that brought me to Málaga. I fell in love with the city and the people I met here, so I've decided to stay and see where this adventure takes me.

When I'm not behind a camera or a screen, I'm probably running by the sea, playing football or tennis, or convincing someone to be in my next video.
